Cubic is a meticulously engineered Point of Sale (POS) and inventory system built specifically for Cubic Billiard. It moves beyond a simple CRUD application, implementing a robust architecture focused on data integrity, observability, and operational reliability.

### Inventory Architecture

The inventory engine is built on an **append-only ledger** model (`stok_logs`), ensuring a complete audit trail of every movement.
- **Consistency**: Uses MariaDB advisory locks (`get_lock`) to serialize stock read-model updates, preventing race conditions during concurrent mutations.
- **Read Models**: High-performance derived tables (`stock_balances`, `stock_movement_totals`) provide O(1) access to current levels, avoiding expensive full-history scans.
- **Negative Stock**: Implements a feature-gated "fast-path" for negative stock, allowing operations to proceed under controlled business exceptions while maintaining strict consistency.

### The Order Pipeline

Orders flow through a multi-stage lifecycle (Pending → Done/Kosong/Cancelled) designed for kitchen-staff reality.
- **Packets**: Supports bundled items that snapshot their components at order time, ensuring that stock deduction follows the exact catalog state that existed when the guest ordered.
- **Financial Snapshots**: Historical snapshots of PPN rates and discounts ensure that financial reporting remains accurate even if tax rates or catalog prices change later.
- **Realtime Feeds**: Leverages Server-Sent Events (SSE) for instant kitchen-to-admin invalidation, paired with TanStack Query for stable, paginated data fetching.

### Systems & Operations

#### Thermal Printing
A custom-built **Windows Print Agent** (`bun run print-agent`) handles direct ESC/POS communication with thermal printers. It features a browser-based ledger to prevent duplicate prints and handles spooler recovery automatically, allowing staff to print receipts directly from the web interface.

#### Observability & Diagnostics
- **Evlog**: A structured event logging system that captures wide events per request, including internal context, performance markers, and user-facing error support references.
- **Maintenance Queue**: A background worker (using Oxen Queue) handles idempotent derived tasks like analytics rollups, search document rebuilding, and automated monthly stock checkpoints.
- **Pre-production Guardrails**: A comprehensive diagnostic suite checks for API auth gaps, business cycle boundary consistency, and residual data drift before any release.

#### Scalability
The system is designed for high-density staff usage, utilizing cursor-based pagination, month-end snapshots for archival data, and DB-level `sort_key` optimization to keep UI surfaces responsive as the catalog and history grow.
